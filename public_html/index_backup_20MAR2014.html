About altiux

Altiux Innovations is an early-stage Bangalore-based end-to-end Product Engineering, OPD and Innovation services company for the Industrial, Telecom, Mobile & Wireless, Consumer Electronics and Healthcare verticals. We specialize in building next-gen products, solutions, platforms and IP in emerging technology domains such as M2M & IoT, Engineering Analytics, Smart Devices & Cloud Media, Mobility Solutions and Industrial/UI/UX Design. We aid large MNCs as well as start-ups in joint IP development, Proto-typing, New Product Introduction, Concept Development, Product Re-engineering and Emerging Market-focused solutions.

Altiux has been founded in 2013 by senior leaders with several decades of experience in product engineering for Fortune 100 MNCs and start-ups. It is funded by BitChemy Ventures, the technology incubation arm of a $1B group with presence across 10 countries and 8 industry sectors.

We are currently building products and IP solutions in the following areas

  • M2M devices/middleware/applications
  • Connected Home & Living
  • Enterprise and Personal Mobility Solutions

Altiux M2M Platform

Altiux CONVERGED CLOUD PLATFORM:

Altiux M2M platform is a single horizontal “Converged Cloud Platform” for Internet of Everything (IOE) - connecting users, devices, apps, processes, contents and services via user’s stationary and mobile devices. The platform can be leveraged to meet all immediate and future needs of smart home, smart connected communities, smart enterprises and other IoT applications. The platform delivers what the smart homes, smart communities and smart enterprises desire to provide in order to enhance productivity, operational efficiency, user experience and what the manufacturing industry & telecom operators want to provide through open standards for convergence in telecom, wireless, home automation, building automation, smart city automation and internet arena in order to enhance operation efficiency and simplify consumer experience.

Altiux cloud platform solves the key problem of connecting physical things with apps, processes, people, content, etc and provide scalable, personalized and aggregated Homespace for consumerization of IT & non-IT services based on single sign-on.

Altiux Connected Device SDK:

Middleware including core agent is home and industrial technology agnostic middleware to connect the emerging Smart Devices based on 6LoWPAN, ZigbeeIP and legacy Stationary Devices based on different standards such as Z-Wave, Zigbee, Wi-Fi, UPnP ,DLNA, Modbus, etc from smart home and smart industries to Altiux “Converged Cloud Platform”. Connected Device SDK also opening new opportunities for our customers and partners such as automation OEM companies (H/A,B/A,I/A) , consumer electronics OEM’s ( HDTV, Set-top Boxes, Router, NAS), ODM’s/ Chip set providers (Z-Wave, Zigbee, WiFi) who want to differentiate their services by adding value added features for their core products.

Altiux Cloud Mobile Connect:

Deployed as native hybrid app or completely HTML-5 app, facilities mobile devices to connect to software platform anytime, anywhere to access, control and manage the personalized applications/services seamlessly and securely. These personalized applications/services are created by Mobile Cloud Application Framework (Mobile Cloud Platform). World’s First Unique UI framework for partners to customize and develop their own branded, scalable and device agnostic Mobile User Interface

Altiux interactive u-commerce platform solution is one unified commerce solution for various content providers. The platform is designed as a ubiquitous - secure, content delivery system, which enables commerce for any content providers, brands and media houses to directly interact with their end customers. iU\C core is industries most innovative state of the art interactive commerce technology backed by powerful proprietary cloud based ‘Customer Behavior Analysis’ (CBA) engine for targeted advertising. iU\C also has proprietary technology for off-the-shelf connected products, which includes Automatic Content Recognition.

Altiux M2M GateWay

The ALTGL1-2G embedded M2M gateway (Motherboard) and ALTSBL1-V sensor daughter board modules allows quick time to market for any site monitoring solutions. Built on simple and secure 2G network, the module helps deliver seamless embedded cellular connectivity platform solution. The gateways and sensor daughter boards host an embedded device manager. This device manager software for ALTGL1-2G Series is optimized for low power and low memory footprint solutions. The network management tools in device manager includes authentication, configuration management, account management. This device manager also seamlessly interacts with Altiux Cloud Platform, and can as well be configured to be used with other cloud platform solutions if needed.
