what we offer

Helping customers build great products is at the centre of everything we do. We provide specialized Product Engineering services across the entire product development cycle, from Product Ideation to Embedded & Hardware design, User Interface design, Cloud and Mobility application development and Machine data analytics.

_________

product ideation

Turning ideas into innovative marketable products require both engineering and creative skills. Altiux helps clients imagine and design innovative products.

With our technology expertise and ability to build products we can serve as your innovation partner in brining your products to life. We adopt a collaborative approach to identify technology bottleneck for growth in your business and find ways to resolve them.

_________

hardware and embeded design

Altiux enables original device manufacturers and service providers to design and test Innovative products quickly and cost effectively.

Shorting product life cycles and rapid technology advances are putting device makers under tremendous pressure to innovate and create a differentiated product. We offer our clients a comprehensive range of services that include embedded design, validation & testing services to accelerate new hardware releases that keep pace with rapid innovation. Our experience and expertise span across all the layers; Hardware, Embedded OS, Media and connectivity middle ware and applications.

user interface design

At Altiux our team of experts in design, HCI, usability and human factors work in close coordination with our team of engineering to build products that excel both in form and function. Our design services include:

Human Machine Interface design and development for industrial, manufacturing & energy applications.
Consulting on Industrial Design – Form, Color, Material, Finish for consumer and industrial products.
Automotive HMI for Infotainment, Multimedia and Connected Car Solutions.
User Experience and Interaction Design for web and mobile applications.
Multimodal Interaction using speech, gesture, haptics and touch.
Wireframes, interactive visual prototyping.
Usability studies, user and field tests.

cloud & mobile app development

Altiux helps customers build both enterprise and personal mobility solutions across iOS, Android, Windows Mobile and custom platforms. Our capabilities and services include:

Mobile enablement services for legacy platforms and applications.
Private App-Store creation, deployment and management.
Mobile application development and testing.
Mobile Analytics.
Mobile UI design.

machine data analytics

Generating meaningful insights using data generated from an ever increasing number of connected devices, sensors and industrial monitoring systems is critical to the successes of IoT.

Altiux offers a wide variety of machine data analytics solutions and frameworks to help clients generate actionable insights from vast amounts of machine and sensor generated data. Our capabilities and services include:
Manufacturing & Process Analytics.
Predictive Analytics.
Big Data Offerings.
Product Analytics.
Technology Focus.
M2M Analytics.
