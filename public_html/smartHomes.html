Smart Homes

Enabling OEMs, service providers and semiconductor companies to rapidly develop and deploy cutting edge smart home solutions

Overview

Smart Homes are arriving at a speed we can barely imagine. Driven by rising consumer appetite for safety, security, comfort and efficiency within their homes, Smart home adoption is already accelerating from early adopters to mass market. Home energy equipment, safety and security systems, intelligent appliances, home healthcare systems, and entertainment devices are no more things of past.


Businesses looking to tap this opportunity understand the key role technology plays in meeting consumer expectations. However, the presence of multiple standards, heterogeneous ecosystems and continually evolving technologies makes it a highly complex market to address.

_________

What we provide?

Altiux provides state of the art software stacks and professional engineering services to enables businesses rapidly bring out smart home solutions. Our offerings comprise of:

Comprehensive, standards compliant software stacks for end nodes and gateway devices that address the key requirements of connectivity, security, interoperability and manageability in smart home devices. Key features of device software stacks include the following :


a. Supports leading smart home connectivity technologies such as IEEE 802.15.4/6LoWPAN, Zigbee, BLE, Z-wave, Thread, etc. and is extensible to support emerging application protocols such as AllJoyn, OIC, Homekit etc.

b. Enables in-built interoperability between devices.

c. Supports end to end security and enables device management using OMA LWM2M and legacy management protocols such as OMA DM, TR-069 etc.

d. Modular, portable, scalable and easily configurable to meet specific customer requirements.

e. Extensible and customizable to support additional frameworks such as edge analytics, multi-vendor interoperability, multimedia capabilities such as uPnP/DLNA, video analytics etc.

_________

Comprehensive software stack for cloud edge that that provides broad support for aggregating data from multiple devices supporting multiple protocols at web scale, and providing a uniform north bound interface for device management, data management and application enablement

_________

Professional services for engineering smart home solutions including

a. Consulting services for defining product specifications, selecting the right technology, training services, and solutioning services.

b. Device engineering services such as board support packages, multimedia middleware, connectivity middleware, application and user interface development along with testing services.

c. Mobility and application services including cloud development/deployment, mobile application development, application virtualization, integration with platform and other backend components etc.
